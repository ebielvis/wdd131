WDD 131: Design Principles

Good Design Principles

Effective design makes content clear, engaging, and user-friendly. Applying principles such as typography, color, and visual hierarchy helps improve user experience.

“Effective web design and art are not the same. But many psychological and design principles apply to websites. You can design a great website by applying the relevant aspects of those laws to your layout, typography, and images.” – Peep Laja

  • Typography: 2-3 fonts max, easy to read
  • Colors: limit palette, ensure contrast
  • Clarity: clean design, whitespace
  • Consistency: same style across pages
  • Accessibility: alt text, good contrast, keyboard-friendly
